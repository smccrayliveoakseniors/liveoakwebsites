About Live Oak Denison

A community built for our community.

We've spent years learning what makes senior living feel like home. Live Oak Denison is where we're putting it all into practice.

Our Story

Rooted in care. Grown in Texas.

Live Oak Senior Care was founded on a simple belief: that the later chapters of life deserve as much thought, beauty, and intention as any before. Our founders spent decades watching their own families navigate senior living — the good, the disappointing, and the things they wished had been different.

So we set out to build something better. A place where caregivers know every resident's story. Where the food is made by people who love to cook. Where families feel welcomed, not inconvenienced.

Live Oak Denison is the next chapter of that mission — and our first community in Grayson County.

Why Denison

Close to home, for the families who belong here.

Grayson County families shouldn't have to choose between great senior care and staying close to home. For too long, that's been the trade-off. We're building Live Oak Denison to change that.

We chose Lifesearch Way because of what surrounds it — an established, growing community where generations of families have put down roots. A place worth staying.

  • Minutes from Texoma Medical Center and area physicians
  • Easy access for family from Sherman, Pottsboro, and across the Red River
  • Close to the shops, restaurants, and services along the Highway 75 corridor
