Care that meets residents where they are.

Two distinct neighborhoods, shared philosophy: every resident is known, every day has purpose, every family is a partner.

Assisted Living

Independence, with a helping hand when it's needed.

Assisted living at Live Oak Denison is designed for residents who want to keep living life on their terms — with just the right level of support layered in. Private apartments. Three meals a day. Housekeeping and laundry. And a caregiver never more than a call-button away.

What's included

  • Private apartment with full bathroom & kitchenette
  • Medication management by licensed nurses
  • Assistance with bathing, dressing, and grooming as needed
  • Three chef-prepared meals daily, plus snacks
  • Weekly housekeeping & linen service
  • 24/7 emergency call system in every suite
  • Full wellness, activities, and social calendar
  • Scheduled transportation to appointments & outings

Memory Care

A safe, familiar place — designed around how memory works.

Our secure memory care neighborhood is built specifically for residents living with Alzheimer's and other forms of dementia. Everything — from the layout to the lighting to the daily rhythm — is shaped by what we know about reducing anxiety and preserving dignity.

What makes it different

  • Dedicated, secure neighborhood with walkable courtyard
  • Staff trained specifically in dementia care best practices
  • Lower caregiver-to-resident ratios than assisted living
  • Structured daily routines that reduce anxiety
  • Small-group dining and meaningful engagement programs
  • Family support, education, and regular care conferences
Dining

Food that feels like home — made by people who love to cook.

Our kitchen is led by a chef, not a warming cart. Meals rotate seasonally, accommodate every diet, and taste the way food is supposed to taste. The dining room is a gathering place, not a cafeteria.

  • Three chef-prepared meals daily, served restaurant-style
  • Rotating seasonal menus with Texas favorites
  • Accommodations for heart-healthy, diabetic, gluten-free, and texture-modified diets
  • Private dining room available for family gatherings

How It Works

Care that changes with you.

Needs evolve. Our approach is designed so residents can stay in the same community — often the same apartment — as their care needs change.

1. Assessment

A thorough, compassionate assessment by our nursing team — before move-in and regularly after.

2. Care Plan

An individualized plan built with the resident, family, and physicians. Updated as things change.

3. Ongoing Support

Care adjusts as needs grow — no disruptive moves, no surprise bills, no guesswork.
