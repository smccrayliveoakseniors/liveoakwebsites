Welcome Home

Quality care, delivered the way we'd want it for our own families.

Live Oak Denison was built for the families of Grayson County — a community where residents are known by name, where days are filled with purpose, and where care is always close at hand.

From our chef-led dining to our dedicated memory care neighborhood, every detail is designed around dignity, comfort, and connection.

  • Assisted living & dedicated memory care
  • 24/7 licensed nursing support
  • Chef-prepared meals in a warm dining room
  • Meaningful daily activities & wellness programs
